About Apadhaa

Apadhaa is a Disaster information tool allows users to be aware about the information of incidents happening around the world at the moment.


Visually display location of the disaster and related information on the map. Users are allowed to search the information on the previously happened incidents by giving the date range. Apadha allows to view news of each incident in the application itself.


Users allows to share the information through social network for the public awareness.
